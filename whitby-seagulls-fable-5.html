Gulls of Whitby

Eee, mind yer chips, pet!

You've just got a lovely tray of fish & chips from t'chippy on Whitby quayside — and every gull in Yorkshire knows it.

Walk with the left-side joystick (or WASD). EAT your chips with the big button. Tap a diving gull or hit SHOO to send the cheeky beggars packing.

Finish the tray and you'll get a fresh one — but the gulls get bolder every time...
